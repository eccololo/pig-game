Game Rules:

  1. When you click button roll you roll number between 1 and 6 which is added to temporary score.
  2. When you click hold button, your temporary score goes to permanent one.
  3. When you roll 1 there is 33% chance you will be ask true/false question. If you will answer correctly you will save collected points but if you will answer wrong you lose temporary points.
  4. When you roll two times 6 you lose all your permanent points and you lose turn.
  5. Default score you have to achive if 200 point but you can edit that at Max Score field.
  6. To start pick max score and click new game. Good Luck :-).
